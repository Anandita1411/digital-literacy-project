Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the source before making payments
- Use strong and unique passwords for accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check bank statements and transaction history
- Be cautious of calls claiming rewards or urgent actions

Financial Safety Tips:
- Never scan QR codes to receive money
- Use only trusted UPI apps for transactions

Report Cybercrime:
- Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
